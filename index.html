Aims and scopes

We believe that the time is ripe to establish in Italy a collaborative network to exchange information and coordinate relevant activities on computational neuroscience. 

The aim of the Conference is to create a fertile environment to foster the next generations of young researchers, share relevant information and scientific advances. Putting together the Italian community of computational neuroscience, more effective actions could be planned to increase its visibility and consolidate scientific exchanges with other relevant communities.
